Bitcoin gift cards are a great way to share bitcoin with friends and family quickly and easily. Redemption only take a few seconds once you have an Airbitz wallet. Follow the simple instructions below.

Redemption Instructions

1

Download Airbitz for Android or iOS


2

Login to the Airbitz Wallet mobile app or create an account tapping Wallets on the bottom bar.

3

Tap Request on the bottom bar.

Then tap Import.

4

Scan the QR code on the Airbitz Bitcoin Gift Card
