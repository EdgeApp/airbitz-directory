Simple Yet Powerful Bitcoin Wallet

Send / Request

Send and receive bitcoin with an interface that is as familiar as online banking.

HD Wallets

Create and manage as many HD wallets as you like. All wallets are encrypted locally and backed up with your account.

Client-Side Encryption

All data is encrypted locally in the mobile app using very strong ENCRYPTION DETAILS HERE and is always stored or transmitted encrypted.

Automatic Backups

All data including all wallets, accounts, and transaction meta data is first encrypted by the app before automatically being backed up to an array of servers.

Zero Knowledge

Zero Knowledge means Airbitz nothing about your account on our servers. This architecture means nothing leaves your computer until after it is encrypted and is never decrypted until it is unlocked with your password on your device.

Open Source

Airbitz mobile app is 100% open source and available for review and use on Github. Open source software means complete transparency for our community of users.

True Financial Privacy

Your financial transactions and financial data are literally your business and your business alone.

We build our wallet to ensure that all of your information is encrypted locally withing the mobile app. This allows Airbitz to put the full power and financial autonomy in the hands of users rather than concentrated in our hands like custodial financial systems.

  • Zero personal information is required to use the wallet (no name, address, email, or phone number required)
  • Local client-side encryption (S-Crypt password hashing and AES256 encryption) of all user keys and data
  • Hierarchical Deterministic wallets with changing addressess per transaction
  • Server data model provides no correlation between transactions and user accounts
  • Open Source and transparent software
