Bitcoin Wallet App (Will be Available Soon)

The airBitz mobile digital wallet is a decentralized, secure, private, synchronized, and backed up Bitcoin wallet. A clean, user-first interface solves many of the usability issues plaguing current wallet solutions, while adding features including:

  • Auto-synced wallets across multiple devices
  • Cloud backups
  • Financial tracking and reporting with exports to popular financial software
  • Simplification of the wallet interface to use user-selected wallet names (vs. cryptic Bitcoin addresses)
  • Local, client side encryption, providing ZERO access to user data by Airbitz servers, employees, or anyone but the wallet user
  • Dynamic addresses change with every transaction to significantly increase wallet privacy
  • Industry standard, incredibly strong, AES256 data encryption
  • GPU resistant "password key stretching" to make brute force password hacking insanely difficult

Bitcoin Business Directory

The airBitz wallet also includes a business directory to help consumers find merchants and websites that accept bitcoin as payment. Some of its powerful features include:

  • Advanced search ability by proximity, business name, category, city, state, or country
  • Inclusion of web based bitcoin businesses such as online merchants and exchange services
  • Fluid interface with high resolution imagery for each business
  • The most comprehensive listing of bitcoin businesses with businesses updated daily
  • Alerts of new businesses in your area accepting bitcoin
